Description

Ginseng Ficus Bonsai is a popular bonsai with a thick, pot-bellied trunk, similar to the Ginseng root. It is considered to be a hardy plant that can tolerate a wide range of conditions, making it a good choice for beginners.

Special properties

  • It is a slow-growing tree, making it ideal for bonsai cultivation as it can be shaped and trained over a long period of time.
  • It has a thick, fleshy root system that is often grown above the soil surface, creating the characteristic “Ginseng” shape.
  • It can be grown indoors or outdoors, but it is the happiest in the bright light, indirect light and high humidity.
