How to choose a bonsai tree?

2023-03-04

Bonsai Tree is enjoyed by people all over the world. When its come to choosing a perfect bonsai tree, there are a few things to keep in mind.

  1. Size Matters: When it comes to bonsai tree, size definitely matters, bonsai tree can come up with lots of different sizes. Therefore you need to choose or buy one can come up with lots of different sizes. Therefore you need to choose or buy one which is the rigt size for the space you are putting it in.
  2. Consider the Shape: The shape of the Bonsai tree is also important. The plant should complement the shape of the tree. For example, a conical tree would look best in a round pot, while a cascading tree would look best in an oval or rectangular pot.
  3. Style Matters: While choosing a Style of bonsai tree is important. There are different types of styles of bonsai plant you can choose. The four main styles of bonsai plants are:formal upright, informal upright,slanting and cascade.Each style has its own unique requirements.
  4. Consider the price: The price is also on important factor to consider when choosing the bonsai plant. There are many different types of bonsai plants available on the market and they can vary greatly in price. You should choose a tree that is within your budget.

- Aayushree Dahal
