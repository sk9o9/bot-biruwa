About
Bot Biruwa

Nepal's fastest growing company which brings greenery around your surroundings and in your home which supports sustainable development.

OUR STORY

Our company was started from a small business which only sold a small amount of product. We started the company with the sole purpose of making your home environment green and fresh. And now we are selling online for the sole purpose of customer easement and providing the best offer. Our company is made up of the best employee and management team. Our story of growth and betterment has been increasing.

OUR PRODUCT

Bot Birwa is the best ecommerce company which has been satisfying customers ever since. Our product is the best quality product in the market. We only sell the product after confirming the best quality by a professional team. We import and export bonsai plants all over the world. We have always received good reviews from our customers.

OUR GOALS

Our main goal is customer satisfaction through our product with their shopping experiences. We provide people to maintain their own garden which makes them active. We are trying to expand our business as large as possible. We always approach environment friendly practices and provide contribution to the green environment.
